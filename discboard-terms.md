Discboard is free to use with a generous free tier. **Discboard Premium** is an optional auto-renewing subscription that removes the free-tier limits.

- **What Premium unlocks:** unlimited discboards and unlimited album covers across all of them. The free tier includes up to **2 discboards** and **25 album covers** total.
- **Subscription options:**

  - **Annual** — $19.99 (US) per year.

### Auto-renewal terms

- Payment is charged to your Apple ID account when you confirm the purchase.
- Your subscription **automatically renews** at the then-current price unless you cancel it at least **24 hours before** the end of the current period.
- Your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.
- You can **manage or cancel** your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings (Settings → your name → Subscriptions on iPhone/iPad, or App Store → account on Mac). Canceling stops the next renewal; the current period continues until it ends.
