Discboard

Lay out your album covers.

Put on an album and sit with it. Spread your covers across a board, arrange them however you like, then tap one to flip its jewel case open and play any track through Apple Music.

Download on the App Store

Remember pulling LP and CD sleeves out of their cases and spreading them across the floor? You'd put one on, sit with it for a while, set it back down, reach for another. No rush. The floor was the playlist — and looking at the covers was half the fun.

Discboard is that feeling, on your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Pick an album from your library and set it down on a board. Nudge it where you want, pinch it to size, turn it to the angle that feels right. Fan them out, overlap them, tuck a small one behind a big one. Then sit back and look at it.

Arrange, your way

Drag with one finger, pinch to size, twist to rotate — any position, any scale, any angle. Tuck small covers behind big ones with a layer slider, or float a favorite above the pile.

Every cover is a real jewel case

A translucent shell, a ribbed spine, and a sheen that drifts across the plastic as you move. Tap a cover and the case lifts off the board and turns around: title, artist, every track. Tap a track to play it through Apple Music.

Your library, your backdrop

Works with your whole Apple Music library, including iTunes Match uploads. Bring your own wallpaper from Photos, or pick from the bundled set, so every board has the backdrop you want.

On all your devices

Your boards, arrangements, wallpapers, and ratings sync across iPhone, iPad, and Mac through iCloud. No account to create, no sign-up.

Free to use

Discboard is free, with room for a couple of boards and a small collection of covers — enough to get the feeling.

Discboard Premium opens it all the way up: unlimited boards and unlimited covers, plus backup and restore. Monthly ($2.99 US) or annual ($19.99 US), with a 7-day free trial.
